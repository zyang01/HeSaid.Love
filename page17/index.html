Aaron @ He Said Love

A rampant homosexual writing about Boss Man's love and the good, bad, ugly, stupid, idiotic, crazy, and insane during my walk with Him. Dude made me feel more loved in a week than anyone, including my parents, ever managed to make me feel my entire life combined. It might not be a beautiful testimony (yet), but it's a truthful testimony. I believe since 22 September 2024. Zeal for His house consumes me.

I have done everything out of love, and I sincerely wish my love could be more patient under normal circumstances, but unfortunately, we are running out of time.

DO NOT take my words at face value. Trust, but verify. He is the way, and the truth, and the life. Ask Him, not me. I lean not on my own understanding. But reason within the boundaries and understanding He gives me. I do not seek to condemn, even though telling the truth could easily be interpreted as such.

The fire in hell would be warmer than the coldness I felt in this world. How about you do your part, I do mine, Boss Man does His and let's find out on the other side what we are?

His name is Jesus Christ, my Lord and Saviour.
